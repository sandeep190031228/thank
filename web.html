Common Questions

Why do people choose Thankful?

Here are a few reasons we've heard over the years:

1) Thankful is like the forever gifting home for you, your family, your organization or your community. Our platform is bigger than one event or theme, so you can keep coming back for future celebrations and milestones.

2) We care so much about privacy. We promise that your registry is private, your data is safe and we don't send any unnecessary spam or marketing emails.

3) We understand that sometimes requesting gifts can feel a little uncomfortable. That's why Thankful is designed to be kind and considerate of both you and your guests.

Does Thankful work internationally?

Absolutely. We have users from all around the world. You can add gifts from local stores and set gift prices in your local currency.

How come Thankful isn't free?

To put it simply, Thankful is founder-owned and we’ve been financially independent from day one without any outside money. This lets us run Thankful guided by our values with no ads and no compromises on user privacy.

Another important thing to consider is we're not an online store, unlike some of our peers. This means our goal isn't to sell you stuff. Our mission is to provide you with a thoughtful gift registry experience that's grounded by gratitude and powered by easy-to-use technology.

How long is Thankful's free trial?

Our free trial lasts for 7 days to give you time to take a look around. If Thankful feels right for you, you can sign up for a "forever" account with us for just $60USD. See our Pricing page for more info.

How does Thankful work for our guests?

Think of Thankful as a beautifully-designed wishlist where your family and friends can browse for your gifts. You can add physical gifts from stores, cash funds for important life stages, and request donations to the causes most important to you. All on the same page.

Since Thankful isn't an online retailer, your guests have the freedom to purchase from anywhere they choose. Even from another retailer if they find a better price elsewhere.
